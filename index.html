I am a research assistant professor at Kellogg School of Management and Northwestern Institute of Complex Systems (NICO). I am primarily working with Prof. Brian Uzzi. My current research focuses on the science of innovation and designing AI based solutions for problems in business.

I received my Ph.D in Computer Science from University of California, Santa Barbara. My advisor was Prof. Ambuj Singh. Before joining UCSB, I got my Master of Engineering degree in Computer Science and Automation Department from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India. I have spent summer of 2015 at HP Labs as a research intern. In this internship, I have worked on scalability analysis of distributed memory programs on large-scale clusters with Dr. Lucy Cherkasova.
